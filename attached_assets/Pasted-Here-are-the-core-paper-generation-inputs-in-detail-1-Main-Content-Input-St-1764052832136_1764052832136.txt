Here are the **core paper generation inputs** in detail:

---

### 1. Main Content Input
- **State**: `inputText`
- **Type**: `string`
- **Default**: `''`
- **UI Control**: 
  - **Prompt mode**: `<textarea>` (48-64 lines height)
  - **Upload mode**: `<textarea>` with extracted content (editable)
- **Description**: Primary text content describing the research topic, methodology, findings, or full document text.

---

### 2. Template Selection
- **State**: `selectedTemplate`
- **Type**: `string`
- **Default**: `'THESIS'`
- **Options**: `'THESIS'` | `'CONF_PAPER'`
- **UI Control**: Clickable card selector with icons
- **Description**: Determines paper structure (single-column thesis vs. two-column IEEE conference).

---

### 3. Target Length
- **State**: `targetPages`
- **Type**: `string`
- **Default**: `'Auto'`
- **Options**: `'Auto'` | `'1-2 Pages'` | `'3-5 Pages'` | `'6-10 Pages'` | `'10+ Pages'`
- **UI Control**: Button grid (2 columns mobile, 3 desktop)
- **Description**: Controls content volume and expansion depth.

---

### 4. Tone & Style
- **State**: `selectedTone`
- **Type**: `string`
- **Default**: `'Academic'`
- **Options**: `'Academic'` | `'Professional'` | `'Essay'` | `'Creative'`
- **UI Control**: Button grid (2 columns)
- **Description**: Adjusts writing voice, vocabulary, and sentence structure.

---

### 5. Reference Style
- **State**: `referenceStyle`
- **Type**: `string`
- **Default**: `'Auto'`
- **Options**: `'Auto'` | `'Harvard'` | `'IEEE'`
- **UI Control**: Button grid (3 columns)
- **Description**: Citation format. Auto selects Harvard for Thesis, IEEE for Conference.

---

### 6. Image Generation
- **State**: `generateImages`
- **Type**: `boolean`
- **Default**: `true`
- **UI Control**: Toggle button (Enabled/Disabled)
- **Description**: Enables AI-sourced professional images via Pixabay API.

---

### 7. Manual Author Details
- **State**: `useManualAuthors` + `authors[]`
- **Type**: `boolean` + `array`
- **Default**: `false` + `[{ name: '', affiliation: '', email: '' }]`
- **UI Controls**:
  - Toggle button (Manual/AI Generated)
  - Dynamic form fields per author:
    - Name: `<input type="text">`
    - Affiliation: `<input type="text">`
    - Email: `<input type="email">`
- **Description**: Override AI-generated author information.

---

### 8. Custom Formatting
- **State**: `useCustomFormatting` + `customFormat{}`
- **Type**: `boolean` + `object`
- **Default**: `false` + `{ fontSize: '12', lineSpacing: '1.5', padding: '1.5', textAlign: 'justify', textColor: '#000000', fontFamily: 'Times New Roman' }`
- **UI Controls** (when enabled):
  - Font Size: `<select>` (10pt, 11pt, 12pt, 14pt)
  - Line Spacing: `<select>` (1.0, 1.15, 1.5, 2.0)
  - Margins: `<select>` (0.5cm to 2.5cm)
  - Text Align: `<select>` (justify, left, center, right)
  - Font Family: `<select>` (Times New Roman, Arial, Calibri, Georgia)
  - Text Color: `<input type="color">`
- **Description**: Override template default formatting.

---

### 9. File Upload (Optional)
- **State**: `uploadedFile` (triggers `inputText` update)
- **Type**: File object
- **Accepted Types**: PDF, Word (.docx), TXT, Images (PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, WebP)
- **UI Control**: Drag-and-drop zone or hidden file input
- **Description**: Extracts and populates `inputText` from uploaded documents.

---

**Direct Form Input Elements Count**: 
- 1 main textarea (content)
- 6 select dropdowns (formatting)
- 1 color picker (text color)
- Up to 3 author inputs per author (name, affiliation, email)
- 1 file upload input
- 2 toggle buttons (images, manual authors, custom formatting)

**Total Interactive Controls**: 16+ (including template/tone/reference buttons, toggles, and dynamic author fields)
